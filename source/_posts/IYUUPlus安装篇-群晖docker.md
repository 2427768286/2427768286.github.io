<!-- wp:heading -->
## 套件中心安装docker
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>输入<code>docker</code>搜索，安装
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/00p8x9.png" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
## 下载IYUUPlus镜像</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>docker套件安装完毕后，打开docker套件，切换到注册表选项卡，右侧输入<code>iyuuplus</code>，搜索镜像，然后下载；
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/b2w359.png" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
## 选择IYUUPlus镜像</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>切换到镜像选项卡，选中镜像，点启动
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/8903oh.png" alt=""/></figure>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/maax4e.png" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
## 设置IYUUPlus容器</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>如图所示勾选，ip保持默认，最后加上端口号<code>:8787</code>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/yrv207.png" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>绿框内选个文件夹，红框内填写<code>/IYUU/db</code>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/wxb1bx.png" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>端口都填入8787
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/22j9b7.png" alt=""/></figure>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/2427768286/STDM-imgs/images/9hp21v.png" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
